# PTO Policy

## Accrual
- Full-time employees accrue 1.5 days of PTO per month.
- Accrual caps at 18 days unless an exception is approved by People Ops.

## Approvals
- PTO requires manager approval at least 7 days in advance.
- Exceptions under 7 days need People Ops sign-off.

## Holidays
- Company holidays are listed separately and do not count against PTO.
