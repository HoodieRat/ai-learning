Audio AI: Hands-on Transcribe + Summarize

You’ll build a simple workflow that turns audio into notes with a verification loop, so you don’t silently miss important details.

Prereqs

  • Audio AI: TTS & STT Basics

Workflow

  • Transcribe
  • Extract action items + key decisions
  • Verify on the raw transcript for critical items

Worked example: meeting to notes

  1. Clip: 2 minutes, two speakers.
  2. Transcribe; highlight names, dates, decisions.
  3. Summarize into three sections: summary, actions, open questions.
  4. Verify: locate transcript lines for each action item.

Mini build: repeatable notes

  1. Pick a 1–3 minute clip.
  2. Define your note template (summary, actions, questions).
  3. Run transcript → summary; link each action to a transcript line.
  4. Re-run on a second clip to ensure the template still fits.

Practice Lab

Task: Convert a short recording into structured notes.

Do: Use a 1–3 minute clip (meeting, lecture, voice memo). Produce notes with: summary, action items, and open questions.

Success criteria:

  • Notes have consistent sections.
  • Action items are clear and assignable.
  • You verify at least 2 action items against the transcript.

Verify: Locate the transcript lines that support each action item (no paid tools required).

Recap

  • Structured templates keep notes consistent.
  • Always tie action items back to transcript evidence.
  • Test on a second clip to ensure the workflow generalizes.
