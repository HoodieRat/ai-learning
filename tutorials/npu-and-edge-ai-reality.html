NPUs & Edge AI: What’s Real vs What’s Hype

Where NPUs help, where they don’t, and what you can realistically do without enterprise gear.

Core concepts

  • Memory fit and bandwidth dominate perceived speed.
  • Thermals and power budget cap sustained performance.
  • Pick parts for real workloads, not just peak specs.

Worked example

  1. List workloads (chat, image, TTS) and memory needs.
  2. Map each to CPU/GPU/NPU choices with VRAM/RAM targets.
  3. Check thermals/noise constraints for your chassis.

Mini build

  1. Make a sheet with workload -> memory/TFLOPs needs.
  2. Draft 2–3 hardware configs with cost and thermals.
  3. Highlight the cheapest option that meets needs.

Practice Lab

Task: Choose a target build within a budget and justify it with workload fit.

Success criteria:

  • You capture parameters and outputs for the run.
  • You can repeat the run and get consistent behavior.
  • You note at least one risk or failure mode to watch.

Recap

  • Size for memory/bandwidth.
  • Thermals/noise are constraints.
  • Choose parts for real workloads.
