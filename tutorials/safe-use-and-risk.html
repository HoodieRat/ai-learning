Safe Use & Risk: Privacy, Permissions, and Common Pitfalls

Practical safety: what not to upload, how to protect personal data, and sane defaults.

Core concepts

  • Licenses and usage rights apply to data, models, and outputs.
  • Attribution and redistribution terms vary per asset.
  • Minimize sensitive data exposure and comply with policy.

Worked example

  1. Identify license type.
  2. Check redistribution/derivative terms for assets.
  3. Document sources and obligations for later audits.

Mini build

  1. Inventory assets (models, data, outputs).
  2. Label each with license and allowed use.
  3. Flag anything needing attribution or restricted use.

Practice Lab

Task: Check license terms for one model/asset and document obligations.

Success criteria:

  • You capture parameters and outputs for the run.
  • You can repeat the run and get consistent behavior.
  • You note at least one risk or failure mode to watch.

Recap

  • Licenses define allowed use.
  • Attribute where required.
  • Minimize sensitive data exposure.
